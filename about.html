OUR BEGINNING

ICSpark began with a desire to offer coding programs to students at a younger age. Our founding undergraduate students believed that early education would be invaluable to kickstart a career in CS, or in just developing the critical thinking and technological skills necessary to succeed in any sector in the 21st century.

This program is also inspired by the work of DevLeague and Jr. DevLeague. In 2016, I started out as an intern and later worked as an instructor at Jr. DevLeague. My time there has been invaluable. A special, heartfelt thank you to my mentors Ray Farias, Sean Nakamura, and the many other role models that previously worked at DevLeague. Thank you to the Jr. DevLeague team Natalie Ramirez, Jay Giang, and Mellanie Soriano for constant positive support. And of course, thank you to DevLeague co-founders Jason Sewell and Russell Cheng for providing me with ample opportunity. Thank you especially to Jason Sewell for opening so many doors for me, and providing guidance both during and after my time at DevLeague and Jr. DevLeague.

I hope this program will continue the values of mentorship, respect, kindness, patience, and excellence that DevLeague instilled in me.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide every motivated 6th - 12th grade student with a fun, nonintimidating coding environment and access to coding mentorship.
