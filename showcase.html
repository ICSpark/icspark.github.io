FINAL PROJECT SHOWCASE

Students were instructed to submit a final project at the end of the course. It could be a completed assigned project, or a totally new creation of their own. Although we transitioned to an online format mid-way, several students were still able to finish and submit their final projects. Mentors provided comments for each submission for improvement, but was overall surprised with the effort that students put into their projects despite the challenges that COVID-19 brought this semester. Great job, young coders!
