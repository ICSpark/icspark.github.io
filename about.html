ABOUT

ICSpark began with a desire to offer coding programs to students at a younger age. Our founding undergraduate students believed that early education would be invaluable to kickstart a career in CS, or in just developing the critical thinking and technological skills necessary to succeed in any sector in the 21st century.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide every motivated 6th - 12th grade student with a fun, nonintimidating coding environment and access to coding mentorship.
