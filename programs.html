PROGRAMS

All of our programs are free, geared towards 6th - 12th grade students and lead by undergraduate mentors. No prior coding knowledge is required. Parents and students will attend a mandatory orientation at the beginning of the program semester prior to the beginning of classes. Upon registration, we will contact you by email with more details regarding the specific time and place.

Below you can find information of our classes. In light of COVID-19, we are only offering Intro to Web Development.

INTRO TO WEB DEV

FEBRUARY 6 - MAY 1

Session 1: SATURDAY 9am - 12pm

This is a 3-month commitment program where students will learn introductory web development with HTML, CSS, and JS, in an interactive classroom format. This program is geared toward students with no prior coding or web development knowledge. Students will be provided with the tools and resources to begin coding their own website on their own.

A maximum of 30 students can be accommodated.

Registration for this semester is over. Please follow our social media to be updated on when our next registration will open.
